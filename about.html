About Me






I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ETE) from RUET. With a strong foundation in robotics and frontend web development, I have hands-on experience in designing and building Arduino and AVR-based systems, as well as creating dynamic, responsive web applications.

My technical expertise spans across developing user-friendly interfaces and integrating interactive features using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and AJAX. Driven by a passion for innovation and problem-solving, I am constantly learning and expanding my skill set, with a particular focus on merging hardware and software solutions.

Whether it's coding the next web application or designing a robotics project, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality, efficient results.
